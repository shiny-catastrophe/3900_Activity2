Willow

Willow is a basset hound who joined our study group May 25, 2024.

She will be 10 in January, but still follows a scent trail like she's 5.

Willow is the best snuggler in our group and always reminds us to take rest breaks.

To learn more about basset hounds, check out the American Kennel Club information page.

Luna

Luna is a miniature poodle/Bedlington terrier cross.

She was born during our younger son's 10th birthday party and will be 9 in October.

Luna doesn't believe in sharing, but she and Willow work together to beg for treats.

To learn more about these breeds, check out the AKC information pages for miniature poodles and Bedlington terriers.
